Reflections

So far I am enjoying my experience at EDA. I like how we have a chanel so we can discuss and help each other out and I am pleased that we each get a one-to-one with our tutor every week. EDA is a original coding bootcamp it has grown a lot since it’s first days as an experiment. This gives me the impression that they are a successful and reliable company to study with. I will be engaging with this culture by learning new technology and surrounding myself with people in the industry. My expectations of EDA haven’t changed. From the begining I have always expected to be ready to start my career in web development by the time I graduate. I'm really excited to be involved in this kind of learning environment and I look forward to meeting everyone on the cohort. Yes I am a bit nervous as it is a big change in career for me so I expect it to be very challenging.
