What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?

I am always thought that meditation was a technique to relax. I remember when I went to Thailand I saw all this monks sitting on the temples chilling for hours. I did not realize that there where inside their minds meditating.

What new things have you learnt about meditation?

I learnt that practicing meditation has positive medical results for our health. Also that meta-attention is the secret of concentration

Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?

I am on day 15 of headspace.com and I really enjoying it. I find that breathing is really important in the practicing as Chade-Meng says: meditation is breathing as if your life depends on it.
